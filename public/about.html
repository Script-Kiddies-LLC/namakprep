About Us


In 2015, Na Maka Kindergarten Prep School was established to give children a head start in education. Here, we are dedicated to prepare every child to enter kindergarten with the skills needed for high achievements and the future. Language techniques including reading and writing, math exploration, inquiry, social and cognitive skills, creative movement, music, and fine arts will be implemented in the course. This well-rounded institution is provided to ensure excellence in anyone who is enrolled.

Our Staff
