Mission

In 2015, Na Maka Kindergarten Prep School was established to give children a head start in education. Here, we are dedicated to prepare every child to enter kindergarten with the skills needed for high achievements and the future. Language techniques including reading and writing, math exploration, inquiry, social and cognitive skills, creative movement, music, and fine arts will be implemented in the course. This well-rounded institution is provided to ensure excellence in anyone who is enrolled.

Please let us know if you would like to enroll your child for our summer session or next school year. The enrollment form and your $100 non-refundable deposit are due by December 1, 2016. Due to our waitlist for the 2017-2018 school year, we will be filling our spaces available on a first come first serve basis (starting from the top of the list). There are currently 15 spaces available for the following summer and school year. Forms and payment can be dropped off at Na Maka K Prep during operation hours Monday-Friday from 6:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m. to reserve your child's spot. Mahalo and have a great day!
