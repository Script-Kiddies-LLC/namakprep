Mission

Na Maka K-Prep School is dedicated to providing a high quality/enrichment educational opportunity for children ages 2 - 5 years old.
We instill a lifetime love of learning and development of value for themselves, their family, and our community.
We provide a well-rounded education through language (reading and writing), math exploration, inquiry, social and cognitive skills,
creative movement, music, and fine arts.
